# 2026-Austin-Public-Library-Computer-Engineering-Public-Seminar

Hello! We plan on giving you an introduction to computer engineering in a way that we have not seen commonly demonstrated before. We are a group of High school students and founders of our schools computer engineering club. We want to bring our love and knowledge of computer engineering to the public by hosting an informal lecture over August 3rd and 4th from 1pm - 5pm at John Gillum Austin Public Library (2505 Steck Ave, Austin, TX 78757).

Again, please bring a computer with ModelSim installed!!!!!!

This is the repo for the 2026 Public Computer Engineering seminar at the Austin Public Library. Here you will see all of our sources and reaserch, the schedule and times, as well as any other information required for attending or else.

Planned topics - Hardware / software abstraction, Files / FileSystems, Coding Languages, Compilers, ISAs, Basic Computer Architecture, Cpu, Types of Memory, Out of order cpus, Gpus, Execution Units, VLSI, and FPGAs.
